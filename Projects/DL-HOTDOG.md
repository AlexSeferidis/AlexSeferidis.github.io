# VAE + DDPMs for Hotdog-Image Generation

**Jan. 2026 - Feb. 2026**

[GitHub Repo](https://github.com/AlexSeferidis/DL-Coursework)

## Synopsis

A deep-learning coursework project exploring generative modelling for hot dog image generation. The project involved designing and training a custom Variational Autoencoder, integrating the pretrained Stable Diffusion VAE, and implementing a U-Net-based Denoising Diffusion Probabilistic Model that generates images within a compressed latent space.

## Skills

Below are the skills and experiences gained from the project:

* Knowledge of generative modelling principles and latent representations
* Practical experience designing and training Variational Autoencoders
* Practical experience implementing and training diffusion models
* Competency with PyTorch and deep-learning development workflows
* Experience evaluating and visualising generated images and learned latent spaces
